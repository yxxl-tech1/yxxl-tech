Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2024

Beijing Yunxiang Xinglan Technology Co., Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile applications, games, and website (collectively, the "Services").

1. Global Compliance & Data Adaptation

Our Services are designed to comply with global privacy regulations and data protection standards:

  • GDPR (EU): We process data of EU residents based on consent, contract necessity, or legitimate interests. We support the right to erasure, portability, and access.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): We provide California residents the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information.
  • PIPL (China): We comply with Personal Information Protection Law regarding cross-border data transfer and sensitive information processing.
  • LGPD (Brazil): We adhere to the principles of purpose, adequacy, and necessity in data processing.

2. Privacy Data Management

We collect and adapt our data processing based on the following categories:

  • Identifiers: Device IDs (IDFA/GAID), IP addresses, and unique installation tokens.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with app features, session duration, and game progression.
  • Payment Data: Handled securely via platform providers (Apple/Google). We do not store credit card details directly.
  • Sensitive Data: We do not collect "sensitive" data (health, religion, etc.) unless explicitly required by a specific feature and with explicit consent.

3. Global Age Policy & Children's Protection

We implement strict age-based adaptations to ensure compliance with global children's privacy laws (COPPA, GDPR-K, etc.):

  • Age Gates: In apps where required, we implement neutral age gates to determine the user's age.
  • Children Under 13 (or 16 in EU): For users identified as children, we do not collect personal information, disable behavioral advertising, and limit social features.
  • Parental Consent: If we inadvertently collect data from a child, we will delete it immediately upon notice at support@yxxl-tech.com.
  • Store Ratings: Our apps are rated according to platform standards (ESRB, PEGI, IARC) to help parents make informed choices.

4. Platform & Ad Network Adaptation

We strictly follow the policies of major ecosystems:

  • Apple/Google: Adherence to App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and Data Safety declarations.
  • Monetization: Our ad partners (AdMob, Meta, etc.) are required to respect user privacy choices, including "Limit Ad Tracking" settings.

5. Your Rights & Contact

To exercise your data rights or for any privacy inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at:
